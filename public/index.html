Welcome to Jumbo Habits 🧠💭

Jumbo Habits is a wellness web app built for Tufts students, designed to help you stay mindful, build habits, and reflect daily. Whether you're trying to improve your sleep, track your study sessions, or just need a space to journal your thoughts, Jumbo Habits is here for you.

  • 📝 Reflect in your daily journal
  • 📊 Track habits like sleep, water intake, studying, and exercise
  • 🌼 Receive affirmations and words of encouragement
  • 📚 Access helpful mental health and academic support resources
